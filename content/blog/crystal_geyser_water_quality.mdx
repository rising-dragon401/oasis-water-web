## Crystal Geyser Overview

**Oaisys score:** 65 / 100

**Owner and Manufacturer**: CG Roxane, LLC

**Treatment process**: micron filtration, ozonation

**Source**: Crystal Geyser Spring's parent company owns springs located in: Weed, California;
Olancha, California; Norman, Arkansas; Benton, Tennessee; Salem, South Carolina;
Moultonborough, New Hampshire; and Johnstown, New York.

**pH level**: 6.7-7.8

**Flouride level**: 0.7 ppm
